<h2 align="center">Executive Business Context</h2>

<p align="center" width="1000">
Healthcare organizations operate in increasingly complex environments where operational inefficiencies, revenue leakage, and poor visibility into KPIs directly impact financial sustainability and patient outcomes.
<br><br>
The <strong>Healthcare KPI Performance Analytics System</strong> centralizes clinical, financial, and operational data into a unified analytics platform. It enables leadership to monitor performance in real time, identify bottlenecks, and make informed strategic decisions.
</p>
